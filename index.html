About Us

Africa's first and only downhill toboggan track

Our Story

Cool Runnings is the first downhill tobogganing track in Africa. Based in the Durbanville hills of Cape Town, this unique family adventure park boasts an intricate course of steel tracks and top-notch wheeled bobsleds.

Fun for kids and adults alike, the combination of speed and skill makes it a thrilling challenge for even the most die-hard speed freak.

How It Works

Starting at the foot of Durbanville's slopes, you settle into your toboggan and the cables haul you up to the start. Once at the top — it's just you, the toboggan, the brake lever, and a spectacular view of Cape Town. Cruise through 17 corners, S-bends and a tunnel before the sled automatically slows at the bottom.

The Ride

Seven steps from ticket booth to well-earned cold one

1

Arrive & Buy Tickets

Head to our ticket office or book online (save 10%). Grab your tokens and head to the track base.

2

Board Your Toboggan

Settle into your wheeled bobsled. Each sled has a simple brake lever — you're in full control.

3

Cables Haul You Up

The cable system pulls you to the top. Sit back and enjoy the Cape Town view.

4

The Summit View

Just you, your toboggan, the brake lever, and a breathtaking panoramic view.

5

The Run — 17 Corners & a Tunnel

Release the brake. 17 corners, S-bends, a tunnel — pure downhill speed.

6

Automatic Slowdown

The sled automatically slows at the bottom. Safe, controlled, grinning.

7

Celebrate at the Bar

Head to our licensed bar — you've earned it. Then do it all again!
